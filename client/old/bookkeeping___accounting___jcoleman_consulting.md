Accounts Payable

Collections / Accounts Receivable

Bank Accounts

Online Expense Reporting

Audit Preparation

As you scale, investors and banks may require audited financial statements.  We can help!

We are experienced in preparing everything needed for the audit including:

- financial statements
- trial balances and supporting documents
- tax returns
- incorporated entity documents
- employment agreements
- financing documents
- other needed records

Having all documents and systems organized and easily accessible will make the process as simple and painless as possible.

Payroll Services

Our bookkeeping and accounting team will work with you to setup an online payroll solution that supports the employees you have today and those you'll add in the future.  The solutions we provide will support your company growth and all state and federal compliance concerns.  In addition we can help support:

- managing time & attendance
- payroll policies and procedures
- payroll data entry
- applying any R&D tax credits to receive immediate cash flow pickup against payroll tax liabilities
- benefits in payroll
- payroll tax reports

[Due Diligence](https://jcolemanconsulting.com/bookacc/#el-38918a84)

Due diligence services for funding

Whether you are the startup owner seeking funding or an investor considering an investment in a startup, we can provide the services needed to deliver the financial report  and recommendations around software tools and internal controls over financial reporting.  Our report typically includes:

- monthly financials (income statement, balance sheet, cash flow statement, etc.)
- bookings
- ARR analysis
- inventory
- deferred revenue projections
- cash runway
- burn rate
- variance reports
- notification of any red flags to look into further
- tax compliance
- salaries / org structure
- non-standard employees
